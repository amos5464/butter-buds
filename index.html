Our Story

Meet Esther
The Heart Behind Every Creation

Esther Afolabi is Abuja's beloved baker — a passionate creator who has turned her love for baking into a flourishing business. From stunning celebration cakes to crispy small chops, refreshing zobo, creamy yoghurt, and unforgettable catered events, everything Esther makes carries a signature touch of warmth and excellence.

She also opens her kitchen to aspiring bakers and food entrepreneurs, sharing her skills with those who want to learn the art of baking and delightful food-making.
